About CardioGuard AI

Our Mission

At CardioGuard AI, we believe that early detection saves lives. Our mission is to make advanced heart disease screening accessible to everyone, everywhere, using the power of artificial intelligence.

The Science Behind

Our prediction model is built on:

  • Dataset: UCI Heart Disease Dataset (1000+ patient records)
  • Algorithm: Logistic Regression with StandardScaler normalization
  • Accuracy: 85%+ on test data
  • Features: 13 clinical parameters analyzed

Important Disclaimer

CardioGuard AI is a screening tool designed to assess heart disease risk based on provided health parameters. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions regarding a medical condition.
